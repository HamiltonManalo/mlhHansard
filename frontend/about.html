Why?

Parliamentary debates are usually long and include complex and specialised language which, for the general public, are often difficult to understand.

Aims

This project aims to solve the problem by summarising and presenting debates in a simpler way.

General method

Essentially, debates are taken from Hansard, the official record of debates in U.K., then using Machine Learning the text is summarised and finally presented through a web interface.
This tool will allow anyone to quickly and easily understand the main ideas discussed in a specific debate
